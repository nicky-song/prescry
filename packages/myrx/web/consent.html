PRESCRYPTIVE HEALTH

INFORMED CONSENT, AUTHORIZATION AND RELEASE OF CLAIMS

I hereby acknowledge that I have registered with Prescryptive Health, Inc. ("Prescryptive") for purposes of: (1) scheduling an appointment for receiving Third-party Services at a pharmacy, clinic, hospital, physician office or any other authorized location ("Third-Party Service Location") via Prescryptive's online booking system; and (2) receiving information regarding my Third-Party Services on Prescryptive's online booking system.

 

Third-Party Services

As used herein, the term "Third-Party Services" means the healthcare services that I am able to schedule at a Third-Party Service Location upon registering with Prescryptive Health. I understand and agree that: (1) such healthcare services will be limited to those services that are within the Third-Party Service Location's scope of practice based on their credentials and jurisdiction; and (2) that Prescryptive may, in its sole discretion, add or remove certain health services from its list of services that I am able to schedule via Prescryptive's on-line booking system.

 

Consent Form

I understand that I will be required to complete and sign a Consent Form at the time of my appointment for each Third-Party Service that I schedule via Prescryptive's online booking system. I hereby represent that I will complete and execute the Consent Form either prior to or upon arrival at the Third-Party Service Location. I understand that I will not be allowed to receive the Third-Party Service until I complete and sign the Consent Form.

In the event that I make an appointment for a Third-Party Service for someone other than myself, I will advise that person of the need to complete and execute the Consent Form prior to receiving the Third-Party Service.

 

Authorization to Disclose My Test Records

I hereby acknowledge that, when registering with Prescryptive, I accepted Prescryptive's Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy and, in doing so, I appointed Prescryptive as my authorized representative to: (1) obtain from my healthcare providers my medical records, including Protected Health Information ("PHI") subject to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act ("HIPAA"); and (2) to disclose and use that information only for the purpose of providing the services that I have requested and in accordance with my instructions.

Pursuant to, and consistent with my appointment of Prescryptive as my authorized representative, I hereby authorize Prescryptive, the Third-Party Service Location and my other healthcare providers to disclose the following information to each other and to any public health authority in accordance with applicable laws, regulations and guidance: (1) my answers to any required pre-Third-Party Service questionnaire; (2) the specifics regarding the Third-Party Service I receive and the date(s) I received the Third-Party Service; and (3) any other medical records created during the delivery and reporting of my Third-Party Services. The purpose of these authorized disclosures is to facilitate the tracking and recording of my Third-Party Services and to comply with all applicable authorities regarding the delivery and reporting of Third-Party Services. Given the evolving science regarding the Third-Party Services that I may receive, I acknowledge and agree that this authorization does not expire, subject to my right to revoke my authorization as stated below.

I understand that I have the right to refuse to accept this agreement and that, if I do accept, I have the right to revoke this authorization to disclose my medical records in a writing addressed to Prescryptive at support@prescryptive.com. I understand that any action already taken in reliance on my authorization, as set forth herein, cannot be reversed, and my revocation will not affect those actions that took place prior to Prescryptive's receipt of my written revocation.

I understand that there is the potential that any PHI that is used or disclosed pursuant to my authorization may be subject to re-disclosure by the persons(s) or facilities receiving that information and would then no longer be protected under HIPAA.
